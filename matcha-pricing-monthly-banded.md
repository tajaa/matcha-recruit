# Matcha — Variable Workforce Pricing (Monthly Banded)

**For operations where headcount swings hard across the year. Fixed seat base + a monthly headcount band that flexes with you. You're billed for each month as it actually is — no year-end true-up, no surprise reconciliation.**

> Note: at peak headcount in the thousands this is the full Matcha platform, not Matcha Lite (which caps at 300 records).

---

## How billing works

Two components on a 1-year contract:

1. **Admin seats — fixed for the term.** You pay for the HR/ops team that runs the platform. This number doesn't move with headcount.
2. **Headcount band — billed monthly.** Each month lands in a band based on that month's active headcount. You pay that band's flat rate for that month. Cross into a higher band in July, you pay the higher rate in July — and drop back down in November.

No 12-month average. No deferred true-up. Each month is settled in the month.

---

## Why this isn't PEPM

The thing you wanted to avoid was a bill that lurches with every temp you hire. Banding fixes that two ways:

- **Bands are wide.** Hiring 200 temps inside your current band changes nothing. The bill only moves when you cross a threshold.
- **Per-head rate is low.** Because the seat base already covers the platform, the band runs ~$0.80/head at the top of each tier — not $5 PEPM.

Predictable like a flat fee, responsive like usage billing, and you get paid for the peak in the month it happens.

## Pricing

**Admin seats (fixed, annual commit):** $250 / seat / mo · minimum 3 seats

**Monthly headcount band:**

| Active headcount that month | Band fee / mo | Effective $/head at band top |
|---|---|---|
| 0 – 500 | $500 | $1.00 |
| 501 – 1,500 | $1,200 | $0.80 |
| 1,501 – 3,000 | $2,400 | $0.80 |
| 3,001 – 5,000 | $4,000 | $0.80 |

The 0–500 band is the floor — it's always charged, even at 200. That's what keeps 5,000-employee capability from sitting on standby for free.

## Worked example — 1-year contract

Assumes a 10-person HR/ops team (seat base $2,500/mo) and a seasonal curve peaking in Q3.

| Month | Headcount | Band | Seat base | Band fee | **Monthly bill** |

| Jan | 250 | 0–500 | $2,500 | $500 | **$3,000** |
| Feb | 250 | 0–500 | $2,500 | $500 | **$3,000** |
| Mar | 300 | 0–500 | $2,500 | $500 | **$3,000** |
| Apr | 800 | 501–1,500 | $2,500 | $1,200 | **$3,700** |
| May | 900 | 501–1,500 | $2,500 | $1,200 | **$3,700** |
| Jun | 1,800 | 1,501–3,000 | $2,500 | $2,400 | **$4,900** |
| Jul | 4,500 | 3,001–5,000 | $2,500 | $4,000 | **$6,500** |
| Aug | 4,800 | 3,001–5,000 | $2,500 | $4,000 | **$6,500** |

Peak revenue ($4,000 band) is captured in Jul–Aug–Sep — the months it's earned — not deferred to renewal.

## Head-to-head vs. PEPM

Same headcount curve, PEPM at $5/employee:

| | Annual |
|---|---|
| PEPM @ $5 | **$98,000** |
| Monthly banded (seats + band) | **$51,700** |
| **Customer saves** | **~47%** |

The customer pays roughly half of PEPM and gets a bill that's predictable within each band. You collect peak revenue in-month with zero float.

---

## Term mechanics

- **1-year contract.** Seat count is committed for the term; add seats mid-term, prorated to the billing cycle.
- **Band is measured monthly** from the active-headcount snapshot already in your employee records — auditable, not self-reported.
- **No year-end reconciliation.** Monthly banding self-settles.
- **Floor:** the 0–500 band plus the seat base is the contractual minimum monthly charge.
