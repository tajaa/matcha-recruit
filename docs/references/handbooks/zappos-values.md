# Zappos Core Values

1.  **Deliver WOW Through Service:** Exceed expectations in every interaction.
2.  **Embrace and Drive Change:** Be adaptable and proactive.
3.  **Create Fun and a Little Weirdness:** Be yourself and keep the environment light.
4.  **Be Adventurous, Creative, and Open-Minded:** Take risks and think differently.
5.  **Pursue Growth and Learning:** Constant self-improvement.
6.  **Build Open and Honest Relationships With Communication:** Transparency and trust.
7.  **Build a Positive Team and Family Spirit:** Support each other.
8.  **Do More With Less:** Efficiency and resourcefulness.
9.  **Be Passionate and Determined:** High energy and persistence.
10. **Be Humble:** Stay grounded regardless of success.

---
*Reference: Zappos Culture Book and [About Zappos](https://www.zappos.com/about/culture)*
