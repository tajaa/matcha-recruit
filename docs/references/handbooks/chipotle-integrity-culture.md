# Chipotle: Food With Integrity & Team Culture

### 1. The High-Performance Team
Chipotle focuses on building "Top Performers" who are empowered to develop "Top Performers" around them.
*   **Internal Promotion:** Over 80% of management is promoted from within.
*   **The 13 Characteristics:** Chipotle historically hired based on personality traits rather than experience (e.g., Happy, Honest, High Energy, Infectiously Enthusiastic).

### 2. Food With Integrity (Operational Compliance)
Policies are strictly tied to the company's "Food With Integrity" mission.
*   **Food Safety:** Non-negotiable protocols for handwashing, wellness checks (no working while sick), and ingredient traceability.
*   **Sustainability:** Policies regarding composting and waste reduction in the kitchen.

### 3. Career Path Policy
Clear "Ladder of Success" from Crew Member to General Manager and beyond, with defined competency requirements at every step.
