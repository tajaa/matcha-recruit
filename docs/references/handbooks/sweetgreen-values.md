# Sweetgreen: The HEART Values

### 1. Our Mission
To inspire healthier communities by connecting people to real food.

### 2. The HEART Acronym
*   **H**umble: Open to feedback and continuous improvement.
*   **E**mpathetic: Understanding and caring for teammates and guests.
*   **A**daptable: Thriving in a fast-paced, high-growth environment.
*   **R**emarkable: Striving for excellence in every task.
*   **T**ransparent: Honest communication about ingredients and business practices.

### 3. Sustainability as Policy
*   **Win-Win-Win:** A core decision-making framework where a choice must be a win for the company, the customer, and the community/planet.
*   **Waste Reduction:** Employees are trained in composting and sustainable operational practices.

### 4. Career Growth
*   **Lead From Anywhere:** A philosophy that every team member, regardless of role, has the potential to lead.
