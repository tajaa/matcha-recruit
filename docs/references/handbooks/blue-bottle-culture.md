# Blue Bottle Coffee: Precision & Professionalism

### 1. The Blue Bottle Way
Focused on "deliciousness, hospitality, and sustainability." The culture is one of extreme precision and high standards for coffee quality.

### 2. Barista Professionalism
*   **Deep Training:** Baristas undergo rigorous training on coffee science and preparation.
*   **Fast-Paced Environment:** Expectation of high performance and efficiency during peak rushes.

### 3. Sustainability Commitment
Blue Bottle aims to lead the industry in carbon neutrality and waste reduction. Employees are expected to be ambassadors for these initiatives.
