# Waffle House: The People Business

### 1. The Core Philosophy
"We aren't in the food business. We're in the people business." Waffle House prioritizes the human connection between "Associates" and customers.

### 2. Operational Uniformity
Waffle House is legendary for its consistency across 2,000+ locations.
*   **Operational Manual:** Highly detailed standards for food prep, sanitation, and conduct.
*   **24/7 Reliability:** A commitment to being open every day of the year, which requires robust scheduling and attendance policies.

### 3. Disaster Preparedness (The Waffle House Index)
The company has a culture of resilience. Employees are trained to reopen quickly after disasters, making Waffle House a benchmark for community stability.

### 4. Direct Leadership
*   **Management from the Floor:** Managers are expected to be hands-on and visible, often working alongside the crew during peak hours.
