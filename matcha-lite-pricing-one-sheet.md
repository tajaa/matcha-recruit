# Matcha Lite — Flexible Workforce Pricing

**Model: Platform fee + admin seats. You pay for the HR users who run the platform — not every temp who cycles through.**

---

## How billing works

Your invoice is fixed. Headcount fluctuations, seasonal hires, and temp workers don't change your monthly cost. The only thing that affects your bill is the number of HR administrators with accounts.

## What this is not

No per-employee fees. No usage caps tied to headcount. No surprise invoices when you bring on 200 temps in July. Your HR team stays the same size; your bill stays the same.
