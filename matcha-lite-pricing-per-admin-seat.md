# Matcha Lite — Per Admin Seat Pricing

**You pay for the HR administrators who use the platform. That's it. Temps, seasonals, and headcount swings don't factor into your invoice.**

---

## How billing works

Each admin seat covers one HR user with full platform access. Employee records — regardless of count — are included up to 500. Add or remove admin seats anytime; billing adjusts the following month.

Minimum 3 seats.

---

## Pricing

| | Monthly | Annual (save 15%) |
|---|---|---|

| Employee records included | Up to 500 | Up to 500 |

## When this model beats the flat platform fee

The per-seat model is cheaper whenever your HR team is small relative to your headcount. A 4-person HR team managing 500 employees pays $12,000/year here vs. $18,000/year on the platform flat fee — a $6,000 difference.

It costs more only if you scale your HR team significantly (7+ admins) without a corresponding headcount increase.
