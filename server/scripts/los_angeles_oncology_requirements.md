- **Regulation Key**: `state_false_claims_acts`
- **Rate Type**: healthcare
- **Citation**: California Government Code §§12650-12656 (California False Claims Act)
- **Description**: Any person who knowingly presents a false or fraudulent claim for payment to the state or political subdivision is liable for treble damages (3x the amount of damages sustained), plus civil penalties of $5,500 to $11,000 per false claim.
